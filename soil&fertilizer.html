Soil & Fertilizers

Build a strong foundation for your plants with the right soil and nutrients

Healthy Soil, Healthy Plants

Soil is the backbone of your garden. Rich, well-drained soil ensures strong root development and better plant growth. Adding the right fertilizers boosts nutrients for maximum yield.

  • ✔ Improves plant growth
  • ✔ Provides essential nutrients
  • ✔ Enhances water retention
Clay Soil

Heavy soil that holds water well but may need proper drainage for healthy plants.

Sandy Soil

Light and well-drained soil, ideal for plants that require less water retention.

Loamy Soil

Perfect mix of sand, silt, and clay. Best for most plants due to balanced nutrients.

Types of Fertilizers

  • Organic Fertilizers (Compost, Manure)
  • Liquid Fertilizers
  • Slow-release Fertilizers
  • Chemical Fertilizers
  • Granular Fertilizers
  • Natural Boosters (Bone meal, Fish emulsion)

Soil & Fertilizer Guide

Type Best For Usage
Compost All Plants Mix with soil
Liquid Fertilizer Fast Growth Water-based feeding
Manure Vegetables Soil enrichment
