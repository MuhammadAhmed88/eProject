Gardening Tools

Explore essential tools for smart gardening

Shovel

Used for digging soil and planting trees.

How to Use:

Push into soil using foot pressure.

Watering Can

Used to water plants gently.

How to Use:

Pour slowly for even watering.

Pruner

Used for trimming plants.

How to Use:

Cut branches at clean angles.

Rake

Used for leveling soil and cleaning leaves.

How to Use:

Pull across soil surface.

Hand Trowel

Perfect for small planting work.

How to Use:

Dig small holes easily.

Garden Fork

Used to loosen and mix soil.

How to Use:

Lift soil to break clumps.

Why Good Tools Matter?

Good tools make gardening easier, faster and more enjoyable.
