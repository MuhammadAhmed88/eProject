Pesticides for Plants

Protect your garden from pests with safe and effective solutions

Why Pesticides Are Important?

Pesticides help protect plants from insects, fungi, and diseases that can damage your garden. Choosing the right pesticide ensures healthy growth and better yield.

  • ✔ Protects plants from harmful insects
  • ✔ Improves plant growth
  • ✔ Prevents diseases
Organic Pesticides

Natural pesticides made from plants and minerals. Safe for environment and humans.

Chemical Pesticides

Strong and effective for controlling severe pest problems quickly.

Biological Control

Uses natural predators like insects to control harmful pests in your garden.

Safe Usage Tips

  • Use recommended quantity only
  • Wear gloves while spraying
  • Apply in early morning or evening
  • Keep away from children
  • Avoid spraying during strong wind
  • Store in a safe place

Common Pesticides Guide

Pesticide Type Used For Application
Neem Oil Insects & Aphids Spray on leaves
Insecticide Spray General pests Direct spray
Fungicide Fungal diseases Mix with water
